REVIEW

Tyler Phillips April 14, 2025

Interstellar is one of those movies you wish you could watch again for the first time. The revalation at the end of the movie brings the whole thing together. The soundtrack is a masterpiece and the acting is amazing.

I've always been fascinated with space with its complexity. The movie shows the frightening nature and the beauty very well. It is my favorite movie of all time.
